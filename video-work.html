Fredo hails from the beautiful island of Puerto Rico. Currently based out of New York, his focus is in digital/analog video manipulation, generative art, and web development.

El Santo

5 min. 2O23

Watch on YOUTUBE

Party Machine by Bruce Haack

El Santo battles some of his biggest foes as they are mixed together with analog video feedback enveloping them in a technicolor lo-fi dreamscape.

Footage was rescanned with a tape handycam, the output was plugged into a video mixer with a feedback of the same mixer being fed to itself as the B input. Using the built-in effects to morph the video results in a kaleidoscope of colors and violence.
