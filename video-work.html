Welcome to the digital archive of wil-fredo. Inside you’ll find projects created using different mediums to explore image distortion, music/visualizer pairings, and looped movements. These works were created using digital and/or analog tools.

Wilfredo is a visual artist from the island of Puerto Rico based in Brooklyn, NY. His focus is in digital video manipulation and music visualizer pairings made with TouchDesigner.

El Santo Visualizer

5 min. 2O23

El Santo battles some of his biggest foes as they are mixed together with analog video feedback enveloping them in a technicolor lo-fi dreamscape.

Footage was rescanned with a tape handycam, the output was plugged into a video mixer with a feedback of the same mixer being fed to itself as the B input. Using the built-in effects to morph the video results in a kaleidoscope of colors and violence.

This project was created using equipment and knowledge provided by Phase Space NYC.
