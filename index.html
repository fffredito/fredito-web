ABOUT

WILFREDO HERRERA comes from the vibrant and sunny island of Puerto Rico. Raised on Cartoon Network, Pokémon and MySpace, Fredo grew to love all things off-kilter. He traveled to the distant land of Rhode Island to study his favorite subject: film. There, he discovered a treasure trove of knowledge shared by others also obsessed with the idea of making movies. Fredo has worked in production in various roles, including as cinematographer for the short film DYNAMITE ABRAHAM: A DOCUMENTARY shot on Super 8mm, as well as editor and director of the music video for the single THUNDER by the Boston band Pyramid Thieves.

Fredo enjoys discovering music and creates mixes under the pseudonym fredonaut



He currently lives in New York City, focusing on producing short films, digital/analog video manipulation, generative art, and web development. He welcomes all artistic collaborations.
