Welcome to the digital archive of projects made by wilfredo (pronounced wil-freh-doh). These works were created using different digital and analog mediums exploring image distortion, looped movements, and music/visualizer pairings.

Fredo hails from the beautiful island of Puerto Rico. Currently based out of New York, his focus is in digital/analog video manipulation, generative art, and web development.
